WoMingbai.com is a project of two foreigners on their mission to master Chinese. Sasha being a studied professional already, Marcus is developing new ideas that will help you learning this difficult language with every step he takes conquering Mandarin. Jia, Marcus’ wife, is a Chinese girl from Tianjin and she will be the voice of this app, so you can listen to real native Chinese pronunciation.

Simply press on their heads in Character details view and you will get the real meaning of this character (Sasha’s head), the ideas behind the image to help you remembering the character (Marcus’ head) or the original native Mandarin Chinese sound of the character (Jia’s head). Being two foreigners ourselves we know exactly where you will have problems when starting with Chinese (because we have been there already). To assure a high quality of our products we cooperate with the „Live the language“Mandarin Chinese School in Beijing, China. All our materials are being checked by professional native speaking „Chinese as a second language“ teachers from www.livethelanguage.cn.

Find more materials for studying Chinese on www.womingbai.com.
